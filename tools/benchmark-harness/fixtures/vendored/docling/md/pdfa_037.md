## Directions to the Maynard Rod and Gun Club 45 Old Mill Rd Maynard, MA. 01754

These directions are written to eliminate having to turn LEFT off of RT-117 on to  (North RD, Sudbury/Waltham Street Maynard.) That can be really dangerous.  It will also eliminate the chance of getting on the wrong section(s) of Old Mill Rd.

## From: First Parish Church, Framingham:

Go North on Edgell Rd, turn RIGHT on Boston Post Road (RT-20) and a quick LEFT to Union Ave. Follow to end and turn LEFT on Concord Rd, Toward Sudbury Center.   In Sudbury Center continue STRAIGHT on CONCORD RD. to PANTRY RD. (Where Concord RD turns right, you keep going STRAIGHT.) At the traffic lights at Rt-117, Turn LEFT.  Turn RIGHT at St. Johns Lutheran Church (NORTH RD. (Sudbury).  This will become Waltham Street when you enter MAYNARD.  Old Mill Rd will be a half mile up the street on the RIGHT. Look for the orange and black Maynard Rod and Gun Club Sign on the telephone pole at the corner of Old Mill Rd and Waltham Street.  (Its easier to see it at night when it lit.) Old Mill Rd is a very narrow street, play nice with on coming traffic.   Follow the street to the RIGHT into the Rod and Gun Parking Lot.  The Pavilion is the large building down the hill.

## From Rt. 495:

Take the exit for RT 117 East to Stow, Turn LEFT that the lights at the end of the exit ramp. Continue on RT-117 East for about 5 miles. until the traffic lights after you pass Erickson's Ice Cream . Bear LEFT at these lights on to RT-62 (Main Street) (Mobil Gas Station).  Follow Main Street to the next lights at McDonalds. (go slow outdoor dining is going on here.) and turn RIGHT (this is Waltham Street.). Get in the left side of the lane and at the NEXT LIGHTS (intersection of Waltham and Power Mill Road) (7-11 and a BP gas station). Continue STRAIGHT up the hill. Old Mill Rd will be about a half mile up on your LEFT, (Look for the orange and black Maynard Rod and Gun Sign on the telephone pole at the corner of Old Mill Rd and Waltham Street.  Old Mill Rd is a narrow street, play nice. Turn LEFT on to Old Mill Rd and follow it to the RIGHT turn into the Rod and Gun Parking Lot.  The Pavilion is the large building down the hill.

## From the intersection of Rt. 128 and Mass Pike

Take Route 20 East toward Waltham. Turn LEFT at the lights at Stow Street, at the traffic lights on Rt 117 Turn LEFT. Follow RT-117 West for 9 ½ miles till you see St. Johns Lutheran Church and turn RIGHT on to North Rd.  (North RD, Sudbury becomes Waltham Street, Maynard when you cross the town line).  Old Mill Road will be about one-half mile up the street on your RIGHT. (Look for the orange and black Maynard Rod and Gun Club Sign on the telephone Pole at the corner of Old Mill Rd and Waltham St.) Old Mill Rd is a narrow street, play nice. Turn RIGHT on to Old Mill Road and follow to the RIGHT into the Rod and Gun Parking Lot. The Pavilion will be the large building down the hill.
